# Shopify-sales-and-customer-analysis
This Shopify Sales and Customer dashboard provides a snapshot of overall business performance by displaying key KPIs such as total customers, single-order customers, repeat customers, total quantity sold, and net sales. It visualizes net sales by product type, payment gateway, billing province, and city, enabling quick identification of top-selling products, preferred payment methods, and high-performing regions. Interactive filters allow users to drill down by gateway, order number, customer, product type, and city for deeper analysis.
